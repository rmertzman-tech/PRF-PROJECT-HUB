The Personal Reality Framework Project

A journey of self-discovery toward practical wisdom.

Welcome

This project is designed to help you map your own internal operating system—your Personal Reality Framework (PRF). By exploring the core components of your thinking (your beliefs, rules, ontology, and authenticity), you can gain a deeper understanding of how you make sense of the world.

The goal is not just self-knowledge, but the development of practical wisdom: the ability to navigate complex situations with integrity, coherence, and a clear sense of purpose. This hub will guide you through each step of the process.

Your Journey in Five Steps

1

Explore Your Beliefs

Start by examining your foundational beliefs about the world, yourself, and your place in it. This is the bedrock of your PRF.

Open Beliefs Explorer
2

Explore Your Rules

Investigate the personal rules and principles that guide your actions. See how your beliefs translate into your behavior.

Open Rules Explorer
3

Explore Your Ontology

Consider your ontology—your assumptions about what is real and what exists. This shapes how you see the world and what you believe is possible.

Open Ontology Explorer
4

Explore Your Authenticity

Reflect on what it means to be true to yourself. Explore the relationship between your inner self and your outer actions.

Open Authenticity Explorer
5

Synthesize Your Framework

In this final step, bring all your saved .txt files together. Upload, edit, and analyze your reflections to create a unified map of your PRF.

Open PRF Synthesizer

Remember to save the final .txt file from the Synthesizer. This document is a snapshot of your thinking that you can revisit and build upon throughout your life.
