The Personal Reality Framework (PRF) Project

A Guided Journey into Your Personal Operating System

Welcome to the Project

This project is designed to help you explore, understand, and articulate your own Personal Reality Framework (PRF)—the unique internal system of beliefs, rules, and assumptions that guides your actions and shapes your experience. By making this framework conscious, you can develop greater self-awareness, personal coherence, and practical wisdom.

The project is divided into two main parts: data gathering and synthesis.

See It in Action: Completed Examples
