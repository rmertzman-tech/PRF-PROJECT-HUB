The Personal Reality Framework Project

A journey of self-discovery toward practical wisdom.

Welcome

This project is designed to help you map your own internal operating systemâ€”your Personal Reality Framework (PRF). By exploring the core components of your thinking, your personal history, and your future aspirations, you can gain a deeper understanding of how you make sense of the world.

The goal is not just self-knowledge, but the development of practical wisdom: the ability to navigate complex situations with integrity, coherence, and a clear sense of purpose. This hub will guide you through each step of the process.

How to Save and Transfer Your Work

In each explorer app, you will have two options to save your summary. Please choose the one that best fits your situation:

  • Save to Synthesizer: Use this for a quick, one-click transfer if you are using the same computer and browser for the entire project.
  • Download Summary: Use this to save a permanent .txt file. This is the best option if you switch between computers or want a personal backup.
